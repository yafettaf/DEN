Our story

“From the Heart of Ethiopia to Your Cup”

Welcome to DEN, where every cup tells a story deeply rooted in Ethiopian tradition. Our journey began with a passion for sharing the rich flavors and heritage of Ethiopian coffee, specifically from the renowned regions of Jimma and Nekemt. These regions are celebrated for their distinct coffee beans, offering bold, earthy flavors with hints of fruity undertones. At DEN, we honor Ethiopia’s coffee culture by embracing its traditional preparation methods. From carefully hand-roasting the beans to brewing them with care, every step is a tribute to the Ethiopian coffee ceremony. This time-honored ritual is not just about coffee—it’s about community, hospitality, and connection. We invite you to experience the authentic taste of Ethiopian coffee, crafted with love and respect for its origins. Whether you're savoring a cup of Jimma’s robust profile or Nekemt’s smooth elegance, each sip is a journey to the heart of Ethiopia.
